About

We are passionate about a secure/peaceful community

Mykemagix Entertainment proudly presents the annual Kwarf Kanival, a vibrant celebration that combines entertainment, education, and social responsibility. Since its inception in 2015, this highly anticipated event has brought together community members, organizations, and stakeholders to foster a secure and harmonious environment in Kwaraffa Quarters and Lobi Quarters, our host communities.

Our Mission

The Kwarf Kanival aims to: - Promote community cohesion and social interaction through engaging activities - Educate and raise awareness on vital issues affecting the community - Showcase local talent and creativity in music, dance, and other artistic expressions - Recognize and reward outstanding achievements and contributions to the community

Event Highlights

- Football match - Award presentations - Traditional Dance competition - Live music performances - Talk shows and interactive sessions - Other engaging activities that promote community engagement and socialization The Kwarf Kanival is a celebration you won't want to miss! Join us each year to experience the excitement, connect with others, and be part of a movement that promotes community development and social responsibility.
